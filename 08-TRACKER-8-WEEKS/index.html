    8-Week Tracker (printable)

    Daily checklist (2 hours)

    • [ ] 20 min SRS vocab
    • [ ] 20 min pronunciation (record yourself)
    • [ ] 40 min input + transcript
    • [ ] 40 min output (speaking + writing)

    Weekly milestones

    Week 1

    • [ ] Can introduce yourself (60s)
    • [ ] Present tense core verbs
    • [ ] 200–300 words active

    Week 2

    • [ ] Basic questions + negation
    • [ ] 2-minute easy conversation
    • [ ] 400–600 words active

    Week 3–4 (A2)

    • [ ] passé composé basics
    • [ ] write a simple email (10–12 lines)
    • [ ] 800–1200 words active

    Week 5–6 (B1)

    • [ ] summarize a short audio
    • [ ] 3-minute structured speaking
    • [ ] 2 timed writings/week

    Week 7–8 (B2 push)

    • [ ] argumentation speaking (pros/cons)
    • [ ] connectors + nuance
    • [ ] 1 full mock/week (timed)
