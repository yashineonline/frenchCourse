# Study Plan by Level (A0 → B2)

This is the “what to do next” roadmap. Follow it in order, and use the prompts pages for output practice.

## How to use this plan
Daily (2 hours):
- 20 min: SRS vocab (Anki or equivalent)
- 20 min: pronunciation + shadowing (record yourself)
- 40 min: input (audio + transcript)
- 40 min: output (speaking + writing)

If you can do 3 hours/day, add:
- 30 min timed exam task
- 30 min correction + rewrite

---

## A0 → A1 (Week 1–2): survival French + clean pronunciation
**Goal:** speak about yourself, ask basic questions, understand very simple audio with transcript.

### Must-master grammar
- être, avoir, aller, faire (present)
- regular -er verbs (present)
- negation: ne…pas (recognize spoken drop of “ne”)
- questions: intonation + est-ce que
- articles: un/une/des, le/la/les

### Must-master pronunciation
- u vs ou
- French R
- nasal vowels: an/en, on, in
- silent endings

### Daily output (minimum)
- 1 × 60–90s recording: self-intro + daily routine
- 1 × 6–10 lines writing: simple paragraph about your day

### Resources (free)
- TV5MONDE A1 exercises: https://apprendre.tv5monde.com/fr/exercices/a1-debutant
- RFI easy French exercises: https://francaisfacile.rfi.fr/fr/exercices/

## A1 → A2 (Week 3–4): control + real-life tasks
**Goal:** handle practical situations (housing, appointments, work), write short emails.

### Must-master grammar
- passé composé (avoir/être) basics
- futur proche (aller + infinitive)
- comparatives (plus/moins/aussi … que)
- object pronouns (starter): le/la/les, lui/leur (only basics)

### Weekly output targets
- 3 roleplays/week (phone call style): ask questions, confirm details
- 2 timed writings/week (email/message)

### Resources
- TV5MONDE A2: https://apprendre.tv5monde.com/fr/exercices/a2-elementaire
- RFI exercises: https://francaisfacile.rfi.fr/fr/exercices/

---

## A2 → B1 (Week 5–6): longer speaking + summaries
**Goal:** 2–4 minute structured speaking, summarize audio, write 150 words.

### Must-master grammar
- imparfait vs passé composé (core contrast)
- relative pronouns: qui / que / où
- conditionnel (polite requests + plans)
- “must know” subjunctive triggers (recognition + light use):
  - il faut que, je veux que, j’aimerais que (don’t over-study)

### Weekly output targets
- 2 opinion recordings/week (3–4 minutes)
- 2 summaries/week (audio → 5–7 sentences)
- 2 writings/week (120–180 words)

### Resources (bridge to authentic French)
- innerFrench: https://www.youtube.com/innerFrench
- RFI Journal en français facile (audio + transcript): https://francaisfacile.rfi.fr/fr/podcasts/journal-en-fran%C3%A7ais-facile/

---

## B1 → B2 (Week 7–10): exam-style argumentation + speed
**Goal:** argue clearly, use connectors, handle abstract topics, do timed tasks.

### Must-master skills
- argumentation structure (position → reasons → example → conclusion)
- counter-argument: “Cependant / En revanche…”
- precise vocabulary by topic (work, admin, housing, health, community)
- speed: reduce pausing, increase clarity

### Weekly output targets
- 2 full speaking sets/week:
  - 1 interaction roleplay + 1 opinion task
- 2 timed writings/week:
  - 1 short email + 1 longer opinion
- 1 mock/week using: [Exam Strategy & Mocks](./09-EXAM-STRATEGY-MOCKS.md)

### Resources (B2 practice)
- RFI B2 exercises: https://francaisfacile.rfi.fr/fr/exercices/b2/
- Easy French (street interviews): https://www.youtube.com/easyfrench
