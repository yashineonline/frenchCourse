# Pronunciation (A0 → A2): fix these early

## The “Top 6” that matter most for intelligibility
1) **u** vs **ou**
- tu / tout
- rue / roue

2) French **R** (throat)
- Paris, travail, vraiment

3) Nasal vowels
- an/en: sans, français
- on: bon, Ontario
- in: demain, immigrant

4) Silent endings
- parler (par-lé), petit (pə-ti), beaucoup (bo-koo)

5) Liaison (basic)
- les amis (lé-za-mi)
- un ami (un-na-mi)

6) Rhythm
French is more “even” syllable timing than Spanish.

## Practice (10 minutes/day)
- Shadow 1 short clip (repeat with same rhythm)
- Record yourself (phone) and listen back
